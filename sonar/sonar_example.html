What is Sonar?

Sonar, LLC is a new web platform enabling independent artists to connect with the resources necessary to thrive in their respective fields. Sonar provides a place that connects and cultivates relationships between multiple facets of the creative industries and brings together the independent creators of the world. By bridging the communication barrier through connecting individuals to a central platform, this new company helps establish connections not readily available outside of this medium.
